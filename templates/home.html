Hello! Welcome to this NHL app

You can view information about the NHL by doing the following:

To see team statistics use the endpoint: /teamID/#.html, where # is the team you'd like to view. Teams are numbered 1 - 31. For example, the Phildelphia Flyers are #4.

To see player statistics use endpoint: /playerID/#.json, where # is that player's ID. Player IDs are 7 digit numberals. For example, Eamon McAdams is ID 8477438.
